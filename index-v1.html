The Challenge

Sim rigs shouldn’t need a workshop to assemble. Too heavy, too long, too ugly for living spaces.

Over-engineered, under-delivered.

Most cockpits are built like industrial frames — great for rigidity, terrible for practicality. We wanted both: aerospace-grade strength with home-friendly design.

Less hex keys, more flying. A system that snaps together, looks good, and scales with your gear.

Our Solution

Modular design, aerospace extrusion, magnetic joints — one rig that fits any mission.

100% Modular

Mix, match, and expand with drop-fit modules for pedals, yokes, and HOTAS.

Lightweight Strength

Precision-cut 4080 extrusion with internal locking brackets — rigid without the bulk.

Living-room Friendly

Clean lines, matte anodized finish, and cable management you don’t have to hide.

Key Features
