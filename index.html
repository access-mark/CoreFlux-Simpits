FAQ

Quick answers
How long does assembly take?
Around 2 hours with a hex set. Modules click in—no drilling.
Will it fit my chair?
Standard office and recliner shells fit the seat rails; bucket seats require side mounts.
Triple-monitor support?
Yes—use the display module with triple-arm kit. VESA 75/100 supported.
Can I mount a keyboard tray?
Yes—side rail accepts our keyboard/trackball plate (STL included).
